Hi, I'm Xuening Xu

I am a fifth year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Stevens Institute of Technology, supervised by Prof. Xiaojiang Du. My research mainly focuses on security and safety of smart home IoT systems, discovering vulnerabilities and seeking solutions to enhance the protection of users and smart homes.

Prior to the Ph.D. study, I received my Master's degree in Computer & Information Sciences at Temple University and obtained my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics at University of Science and Technology of China.
